Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how DeepWork ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the DeepWork app.

1. Information We Collect

DeepWork does not collect or store any personal data on external servers.

All focus session data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

2. Subscription & Payments

Subscriptions are handled entirely through Apple's App Store.

We do not receive or store any payment information.

Apple may provide us with anonymized subscription status information to enable app functionality.

3. Analytics

We may use minimal, privacy-friendly analytics (such as aggregated usage data) to improve the app.

No personally identifiable information is collected.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

We do not store user data on our own servers.

5. Children's Privacy

DeepWork is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

As DeepWork stores data only on your device, you may delete the app at any time to remove all stored information.
